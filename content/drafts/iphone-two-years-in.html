Two years ago I wrote, "[iPhone 3G One Week In](/personal/2008/07/iphone-3g-one-week-in/)". In that time I replaced the iPhone 3G with a 3GS, copy-paste and multitasking have been added to the newly renamed iOS, iPhone 4 has recently been released and Android has risen to be viable competitor to iPhone --- a lot has happened. In the original post I reviewed things like typing on the onscreen keyboard and the web browser. Nowadays these things barely enter into a review of a smartphone. They're common and expected to work well.
